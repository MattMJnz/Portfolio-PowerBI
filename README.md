## Preview
Referral Process Visibility Dashboard on Power BI,
Designed and developed a two-module Power BI solution to provide HR and Payroll teams with full visibility into the employee referral process. Each module was tailored to the specific operational needs of each role, delivering the right information to the right stakeholders. The implementation reduced processing times and eliminated cases where referrals fell out of scope or were overlooked, resulting in a more reliable and trackable end-to-end workflow.
